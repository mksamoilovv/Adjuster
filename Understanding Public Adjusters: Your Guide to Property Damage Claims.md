## 🌟 [What is a Public Adjuster?](https://bit.ly/public-adjuster)

A public adjuster is a licensed professional who advocates for policyholders during the claims process. Unlike independent adjusters, who are hired by insurance companies, public adjusters work solely for you. Their primary goal is to ensure that you receive the maximum compensation for your claim.

### Key Responsibilities of a Public Adjuster:
- **Claim Evaluation**: Assessing the extent of damage to your property.
- **Documentation**: Collecting necessary evidence to support your claim, including photos and repair estimates.
- **Negotiation**: Working with the insurance company to negotiate a fair settlement on your behalf.
